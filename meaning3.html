OM

Core Meaning

"Om" represents divine energy, purity, and spiritual essence, evoking the serene and uplifting presence of angels.

Symbolism

It symbolizes guidance, protection, and harmony, reflecting the celestial aura of angelic beings and the calm of the heavens.

Spiritual Insight

Meditating on "Om" encourages mindfulness, clarity, and connection to higher consciousness, bringing inner peace akin to angelic grace.
