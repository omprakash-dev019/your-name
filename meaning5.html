PRAGYAN

Core Meaning

Pragyan signifies Wisdom and Intellect — the magical ability to see beyond the obvious, seeking knowledge and understanding of the world.

Personality Symbolism

The name represents Curiosity and Enlightenment, inspiring one to explore mysteries, learn, and grow with insight.

Spiritual Insight

Pragyan encourages mindfulness, clarity, and self-awareness, guiding one toward inner balance and personal growth.

Life Guidance

This name symbolizes learning and wisdom-driven decisions in life, helping navigate challenges with intelligence and intuition.
